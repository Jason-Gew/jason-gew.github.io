Blog Starts From Here

Author: Jason Ge Wu
Date: 2020-10-01   10:10:00
Category: Other

Let me start the first article of my blog with the following questions and answers.
They also represent the original intention for creating this blog.

Why I write a blog‚ùì

With the time goes on, professional experience at work is increasing, but also the trivia in life. Sometimes, great ideas come up in mind, but did not get time to implement, and then they just disappeared.
Therefore, it is the time to write them down instead of burying in my memory.

What I am going to post‚ùî

The posts are no only about domain technology, ideas and work experience, but also some wonderful moments in life.
Topics involved in Tech domain:

  • Big Data
  • Cloud Computing
  • New Software Technology

Topics involved in Life category:

  • Travel and Plans
  • Dining and Restaurants
  • Movies

Besides, I will share many other interesting stories as well. üòÉ

When to write and post‚ùì

There is no specific schedule or timeline. However, after a certain period, or have great ideas, or encountered a significant event, I will definitely write a post.

Where to start‚ùî

I will start with the most important, clearest and easiest. In this case, it will be easy to record and accumulate certain details soon.

How to do it‚ùì

It is not difficult to make one post, but it is hard to insist on writing. So it is very important to keep making summaries.
Even if no time to finish all, write an outline for the post is better than doing nothing. I will also try to split the large amount of content into small articles.

Who will participate‚ùî

You of course! Readers are my biggest motivation. üßê
